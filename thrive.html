Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Group Thrive Solutions empowers youth groups, women groups, and community-based organizations in Kenya by providing legal and financial tools to achieve self-reliance, equity, and sustainable development. Through education, advocacy, and resource access, we strengthen communities to thrive and drive positive change.

Our Vision

A Kenya where youth, women, and community-based organizations are legally empowered, financially independent, and actively shaping inclusive, thriving communities.

Our Services

Legal Support Services

Helping groups establish a legal foundation and maintain compliance for accessing resources and effective operations.

Key Services:

Group Registration Assistance

Guide through registration with Ministry of Labor and Social Protection

Constitution Drafting Support

Create tailored constitution meeting legal requirements

Legal Compliance Training

Educate on ongoing legal obligations post-registration

Financial Support Services

Building financial capacity and access to resources for economic independence and sustainability.

Key Services:

Financial Literacy Training

Workshops on budgeting, savings, debt management, and financial planning

Access to Funding Facilitation

Help identify and apply for government funds, micro-grants, or microfinance

Enterprise Development Mentorship

Provide mentorship for starting or managing small businesses

Value: Builds sustainable income sources, reducing reliance on external funding.

Capacity Building Services

Strengthening group operations and leadership for long-term success and impact.

Key Services:

Governance & Leadership Training

Train on effective governance, decision-making, and conflict resolution

Project Management Workshops

Equip groups with skills to plan, implement, and monitor projects

Networking & Partnership Facilitation

Connect groups to NGOs, government agencies, and private partners

Value: Strong governance reduces internal disputes and enhances group credibility.
