SERVICE DESCRIPTION

Group Thrive Solutions is a Kenyan social enterprise dedicated to empowering youth groups, women groups, and community-based organizations through accessible legal and financial services. We provide Legal Support

  • Guidance on group registration processes with the Ministry of Labor and Social Protection, including constitution drafting and compliance with the Community Groups Registration Act 2022
  • Legal education workshops on rights, contracts and property ownership to protect groups from exploitation.

    • Financial Support
  • Financial literacy training on budgeting, savings, and business planning to foster ecomnomic independence.
  • Facilitation of access to government funds (e.g., Hustler Fund,Youth Enterprise Development Fund, Women Enterprise Fund) and micro-grants from local and international donors
  • Mentorship for group-led enterprises to ensure sustainable income generation.

    OUR MISSION

    Empower youth groups, women groups, and community-based organizations.
    Provide legal and financial tools for self-reliance.
    Strengthen communities to drive positive change.

    Legal Empowerment and Protection for Community Groups

    We guide groups through registration processes, draft constitutions, and provide legal education workshops. Our legal support ensures groups have the foundation to access resources, protect their rights, and operate effectively within legal frameworks.

    Financial Independence Through Training and Resource Access

    We deliver financial literacy training, facilitate access to government funds and grants, and provide mentorship for group enterprises. Our financial services enable sustainable income generation and reduce reliance on external support.
